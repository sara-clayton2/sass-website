FAQ

How does the 30 day money back guarantee work?

Purchase any ultimate plan and try it out for 30 days, risk free. Cancel your account within the first 30 days and we'll refund your money right away. Also, you can cancel your plan at any time. Our money back guarantee is only available for first time purchases.

Why Am I Recommended Playlists By People I Do Not Know?

You may not know each other, but your taste in music is similar! We recommend other users' playlists which contain music that should appeal to you. Playlists you create might be recommended to other users too.

How Can I Find The Bill For My Subscription?

Your most recent account activity is available to view on your profile pages. Log into your account on a computer click on your name in the right hand corner of your home page then click My Account.Your subscription / billing information is under the My Subscription tab.

How Do I Add The Track I Am Listening To To A Playlist?

1. Hover on the player box in the bottom left of your screen and click on the + icon that appears. 2. Click Add to Playlist and then select a playlist or create a new playlist to add the track to.
